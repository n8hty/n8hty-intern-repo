# Introduction to Wireframing & When to Use It

## What are the benefits of starting with wireframes rather than jumping into high-fidelity design?
Wireframes help designers focus on layout, structure, and usability before spending time on detailed visuals.
They are faster to create, easier to change, and make it simpler to test ideas early in the design process.

## How can wireframes help identify usability issues early?
Wireframes help teams spot confusing navigation, poor layouts, or missing information before development begins. 
In Focus Bear, a wireframe could reveal if users struggle to find the important features or task flow before adding colors and final styling.

## If a stakeholder asks for a high-fidelity design too soon, how would you explain the value of wireframing?
I would explain that wireframes save time by helping the team test and improve the structure first before focusing on detailed visuals. 
Fixing usability problems early is much easier and cheaper than redesigning a polished interface later in the process.
