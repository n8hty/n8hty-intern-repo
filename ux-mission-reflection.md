# How UX Contributes to Focus Bear’s Mission

# Reflection

## If a new feature looks visually appealing but is hard to use, how should UX designers handle that tradeoff?
UX designers should prioritize usability over appearance. For example, some apps use many animations and widgets that look good but can be distracting. From my 
experience exploring Focus Bear, its simple and low-distraction design makes features easier to understand and use.

## What specific challenges might neurodivergent users face when using productivity apps?
Neurodivergent users may struggle with task overload, distractions, and confusing interfaces. I have noticed that cluttered dashboards and too many notifications 
can feel overwhelming. Focus Bear helps by showing tasks in a structured way and encouraging users to focus on one activity at a time.

## How can Focus Bear's UX differentiate itself from other productivity tools?
Focus Bear stands out by focusing on accessibility and reducing distractions. Unlike many productivity apps that offer many features at once, Focus Bear uses 
simple layouts and focus tools such as website blocking to help users stay on track. This creates a more supportive experience for AuDHD users.
