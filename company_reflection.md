# Understanding of Focus Bear's mission and vision

## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created initially because of the founder's personal struggle with technology overuse, which negatively affected his lifestyle and mental health. 
This inspired him to create an anti-distraction app that would help users, especially people with ADHD and ASD such as himself, to boost their productivity and 
achieve healthy habits.

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
It is helping users with difficulty in staying focused and productive, by reminding and gviing them restrictions that would hinder them from staying committed to 
being productive and making healthy habits.

## Why do you think this mission is important?
This mission is important as ADHD diagnosis are skyrocketing and technology overuse is being normalized, which can negatively impact a person's wellbeing and future.
Focus Bear actively blocks distractions and also guides its user into being consistent in building a healthy routine through its available features.

## How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?
It aligns with my personal values and interest because I know how difficult it is to mantain a healthy lifestyle, while dealing with constant distractions. 
I sympathize with people that struggle to finish tasks, which causes them to self blame and have difficulty in managing work and relationships.
I believe that having a well managed and disciplined life can greatly enhance a person's self-esteem, which can overall boost their health and wellbeing.

## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
I can personally relate to the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve as I was also easily distracted, which affected my personal life and self-esteem. 
Overtime, I was able to change that by managing my tasks and building healthy habits, which was difficult at first--especially without a system that could 
automatically keep me accountable or remind me constantly. That is why I appreciate Focus Bear's mission to provide an easier and structured way to build discipline,
and develop healthy routines over time.
