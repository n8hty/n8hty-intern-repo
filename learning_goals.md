# Set your learning goals

## What do you hope to learn from this internship?
I am hoping to gain real-world experience and design systems that are professionally given feedback. That way I am able to learn more and improve my skills,
enabling me to generate more creative ideas and meaningfully contributing to the company.

I am most excited to develop and apply my creativity and problem solving skills. By having real industry experience I am able to gain professional feedback, which
I believe this will help me further improve my understanding of user perspectives and create better user experiences.

## What do you think you can contribute to the team?
I believe my experience, creativity, and user-centered approach would allow me to contribute well to the company. 
I pay close attention to user flow and always try to think from the user’s perspective. Additionally, my high standards for design and UX push 
me to create intuitive, polished, and meaningful user experiences.

I feel there may be standards and practices that I have yet to fully learn, and I am hoping this internship will help me gain that real-world experience and improve
as a UX Designer.

## What does success in this internship look like for you?
My main priority would be to learn and gain more ideas that I haven't thought about before, and then next 
would be to contribute and gain real industry experience in the company, and finally to complete my required internship hours on time before my graduation. 
If I am able to accomplish all these, I would call it an internship success.
