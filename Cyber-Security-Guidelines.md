# Cyber Security

## What security measures do you currently follow, and where can you improve?
I already use strong passwords and avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily. However, I can improve by using a password manager more consistently 
and regularly reviewing my account security settings.

## How can you make secure behaviour a habit rather than an afterthought?
I can make security a habit by treating it as part of my daily workflow rather than something I only think about when problems occur. Simple practices like 
checking links before clicking and locking devices when away can become routine over time.

## What steps will you take to ensure your passwords and accounts are secure?
I will use unique passwords for each account, store them in a password manager, and enable two-factor authentication whenever possible. 
I will also keep my devices and software updated to reduce security risks.

## What would you do if you suspected a security breach or suspicious activity on your account?
I would immediately change my password, log out of other sessions, and enable or review two-factor authentication. 
I would also report the issue to the appropriate team or service provider and monitor the account for any further suspicious activity.
