# How UX Works with Other Teams

## How do UX designers work with developers to ensure accurate implementation?
UX designers work with developers to ensure accurate implementation and works as intended. 
Developers provide feedback on feasibility, while designers share detailed specifications, interaction guidelines, and design standards to keep 
everything aligned during development.

## How does UX contribute to product decisions and roadmap planning?
UX contribute to product decisions and roadmap planning by focusing on users’ actual needs and experiences. Through research and usability testing, 
UX helps teams prioritize features, improve usability, reduce friction, and create solutions that support both user satisfaction and business goals.

## What role does UX play in QA and usability testing?
UX is important in QA and usability testing because it helps ensure the product feels intuitive, accessible, and consistent for users. Designers review the 
final product and conduct usability tests to identify confusion, usability issues, or areas that need improvement before launch.

## How should designers provide clear documentation and feedback to other teams?
Designers should provide clear and organized documentation using detailed specs, annotations, and design systems. Keeping everything simple, consistent, 
and easy to understand helps developers and stakeholders implement the design more accurately and understand the reasoning behind design decisions.

# Reflection

## How can UX ensure a smooth handoff between design and development?
A smooth handoff between design and development depends on strong communication and collaboration. Clear documentation, regular discussions, and 
early feedback help avoid misunderstandings and make the implementation process more efficient.

## What challenges might arise when design, dev, and product teams have conflicting priorities?
The challenges that might arise when design, dev, and product teams have conflicting priorities can lead to delays, compromises, or inconsistent user experiences. 
Balancing business goals, technical constraints, and user needs often requires teamwork, flexibility, and open communication.
 
## If a developer modifies a UX design due to technical limitations, how should a UX designer respond?
If a developer modifies a UX design due to technical limitations, the designer should work closely with them to understand the issue and explore 
alternative solutions. The goal is to find a practical compromise that still maintains a positive user experience.
