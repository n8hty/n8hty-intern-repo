# Professionalism at Focus Bear

## Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?
I have experienced good teamwork in group projects and during my internship. What made it effective was clear communication, mutual respect, and 
team members being willing to help each other and provide feedback when needed.

## What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful, and inclusive?
I can communicate clearly by being polite, listening carefully, asking questions when something is unclear, and considering different perspectives. 
I also try to use simple and respectful language to avoid misunderstandings.

## How can you help create a positive, fun, and professional work environment?
I can contribute by maintaining a positive attitude, respecting others, being open to feedback, and cooperating with teammates. I believe a friendly and 
supportive environment helps everyone work more effectively.

## How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors and colleagues?
I can prepare by reviewing previous discussions, organizing my questions, and having updates on my tasks ready. This helps meetings become more productive and 
efficient.

## What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?
Being proactive means taking initiative and looking for ways to contribute instead of waiting for instructions all the time. During my internship, I often had to 
identify tasks and learning opportunities on my own when no specific assignments were given.

## How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information/action from them? How would you escalate it further if you don't get a response and when would you escalate?
I would send a polite follow-up message and give them reasonable time to respond. If the matter is important and there is still no response after multiple 
follow-ups, I would escalate it by informing another appropriate supervisor or team member while remaining respectful and professional.

## What are some things you won't do?
I will not intentionally ignore feedback, disrespect teammates, take credit for other people's work, or communicate unprofessionally. 
I also will not hesitate to ask questions when I need clarification instead of making assumptions that could lead to mistakes.
