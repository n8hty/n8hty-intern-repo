# Conduct a Usability Test with a Friend & Report Findings

## Introduction
Thank you for participating in this usability test. We're evaluating how easy it is to use this feature and identifying areas for improvement. 
Please feel free to think aloud as you complete the tasks. There are no right or wrong answers.

## Scenario
It's almost final exams week, and you want to improve your grades and study habits. You decide to use the app to create a study routine and minimize distractions 
so you can stay focused and prepare effectively for your exams. Please use the app as you normally would to set up a routine that helps you achieve this goal.

## Follow-up Questions
1. How easy or difficult was it to set up your study routine?
2. Was there anything confusing or difficult to find?
3. Did the app provide the tools or information you expected?
4. What features did you find most helpful?
5. Was there anything missing that would have helped you prepare for finals?
6. What improvements would you suggest to make the experience better?
7. On a scale of 1–5, how satisfied are you with this experience?
